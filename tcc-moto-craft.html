ABOUT TCC CUSTOMS

Founded in 2005 by Tony "Chopper" Carter, TCC Customs has grown from a one-man garage operation to one of the most respected custom motorcycle shops in the country.

What started as a passion for building fast, unique motorcycles has evolved into a full-service shop specializing in custom baggers, choppers, vintage restorations, and high-performance engine builds.

Every bike that rolls out of our shop is built with the same attention to detail and quality that we'd demand for our own rides. We don't cut corners - we build bikes that perform as good as they look.
